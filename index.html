My flowers are falling off or dying?

+ -

Check watering: Ensure you're not overwatering or underwatering. Assess light conditions: Confirm your flowers receive the right amount of sunlight. Examine soil: Ensure well-draining soil and consider adding organic matter. Monitor temperature and humidity: Keep conditions within the preferred range.

What causes leaves to become pale?

+ -

Diseases or pests can weaken plants and result in pale or discolored leaves.
Inspect your plants regularly for signs of disease or pests and take appropriate measures for control.

What causes brown cripy leaves?

+ -

Discolored or dark spots on leaves. Wilting: Sudden drooping or wilting of leaves. Cankers: Sunken and discolored areas on stems or branches. Mold or Mildew: Fuzzy or powdery substances on the leaves.
